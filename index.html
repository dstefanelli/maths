TOOLS FOR MATH INSTRUCTORS

  • Over 1,100 unique math problems

  • Immediate feedback with worked out solutions

  • A detailed reporting tool that provides performance data on the individual, class, or course level

  • All scenes have been developed through student focus groups and created by math faculty

  • A virtual math palette - accessible when students are solving problems where mathematical symbols and expressions are needed (makes inputting answers easy)

  • At the end of each module our “Additional Exercises” will correspond with the problems from your specific textbook

  • A unique gaming feature challenges students by ranking the top five scores for each module

  • Customize the order of the modules to fit any textbook you currently use

  • The custom calendar software allows you to start and stop each module on specific dates for each instructor in the department

  • Set a mastery score (that must be achieved before progressing) for your specifc students for each module

Features

The Real World of Math was designed with students and instructors in mind. Our tools are easy to use and we offer every instructor one-on-one-training to ensure they understand how to apply them to their style of teaching. Our goal is to give instructors everything they need to connect with their students and be successful in the classroom.

Getting started

We ensure all your students are enrolled prior to each term. With our 24/7 instructor / admin access you’ll have real-time visibility into your students progress within modules, grades, and editing throughout the semester.

Glossary of terms

A Glossary of terms is available for your students at all times. We integrate directly into your Learning Management System (i.e. Canvas, Bb, D2L, Moodle, LoudCloud, Institutionally-built systems, etc.) to ensure a single-sign-on experience.

Create your own modules

Easily convert all of your students data to an excel doc with a few clicks. If were missing something , let us know. We’ll create content specifically for your course.
